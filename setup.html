Setup Syncloud Device

Setting up Syncloud device is easy as one-two-three
1. Get Hardware
Syncloud device is a small box with low power consumption.
buy it or build yourself
2. Connect Everything
Connect your device to your home router. Use one of the available LAN ports. Plug in power wire and turn the device on.
3. Activate
Create account at syncloud.it. Install Syncloud app on your smartphone and use it to find and activate your Syncloud device. Syncloud app is simple and intuitive, watch this demo video.
4. Now you are ready!
Get to your device by the domain name. Install Syncloud apps in App Center of your device with one click! Activate external disk and make device available through Internet at Settings page of the device.
