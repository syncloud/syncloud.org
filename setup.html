Setup Syncloud Device

Setting up Syncloud device is easy as one-two-three
1. Get Hardware
First check things to know before buying a device. Syncloud device is a small box with low power consumption.
buy it or build yourself
2. Connect Everything
Connect your device to your home router. Use one of the available LAN ports. Plug in power wire and turn the device on.
3. Activate
Open device IP in your brawser and follow the activation process, watch this demo video . More details about activation process can be found here.
4. Now you are ready!
Get to your device by the domain name. Install Syncloud apps in App Center of your device with one click! Activate external disk and make device available through the Internet at Settings page of the device.
